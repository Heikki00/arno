**Commands**
*!play url times seek*: Adds an youtube video to the queue
    *url*: The link to the video
    *times*: How many times the video should be played (optional, default 1)
    *seek*: Where the video should be started, in seconds from start(optional, default 1)

*!volume x*: Changes volume
    *x*: New volume, can be a number from 0 to 200

*!skip*: Skips the current video

*!stfu*: Shuts the bot the fuck up, also empties the queue
